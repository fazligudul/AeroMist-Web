About us

Our Story

From a Bilkent classroom to smart indoor growing.

AeroNex Labs was formed as Team 11 in the context of Bilkent University course sequence GE 401 and GE 402—integrating entrepreneurial process, product development, and engineering design. We identified a gap in the Turkish market for accessible, smart aeroponic systems and committed to building AeroMist from concept to a validated MVP.

Our values

  • Innovation

    Technology and design that make growing simpler and more reliable.

  • Sustainability

    Less water, no soil, and efficient use of resources.

  • User-first

    Automation and clarity so anyone can grow at home.

  • Rigor

    From QFD and PTS to prototyping and testing, we build on solid foundations.
