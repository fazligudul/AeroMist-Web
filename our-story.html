Our Story

We are a hands-on team that thinks like growers: close to nature, careful with water, and focused on practical, responsible food production.

Water · growth · care

Est. 2025

Why we built AeroMist

We started with a simple question: why should growing fresh herbs at home be messy, space-heavy, or expensive? That led us to AeroMist—a compact, sensor-driven aeroponic system for your counter: no soil, minimal water, and full control from your phone.

  • Countertop footprint—no soil, minimal water
  • Sensors and app control you can trust
  • From first sketches to a user-tested prototype

Why this matters to us

We’re also the kind of team that loses sleep over how essential resources—especially water—are depleted and too often wasted. Protecting the environment and helping people access fresh, natural produce aren’t side notes on our roadmap; they’re why we chose this problem in the first place.

Our values
