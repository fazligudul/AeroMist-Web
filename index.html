How to use AeroMist

  1. 1

    Connect your Android phone

    Get the AeroMist app on Google Play, sign in, and connect your unit over Wi‑Fi. Sensors, schedules, and history stay with you wherever you open the app.

  2. 2

    Set misting & lights

    Pick how often and how long it mists, line up the grow lights with your plants, or switch to Auto when you want it to run itself. Tap Mist now whenever you want an extra burst.

  3. 3

    Monitor on the app or the LED

    See temperature, humidity, water, and mist status on your Android phone, or check the same kind of readout on the built-in LED screen on the front when you are next to the unit.

Features

  • Environmental sensing

    SHT31: temp ±0.3°C, humidity ±2% RH. Updates every 90s.

    Precision sensors keep your plants in the ideal range. Data is sent to the app so you can track trends over time.

  • Water level

    Float switch with low-reservoir alert at ≤20%.

    Never run dry. The system pauses misting and notifies you on the app when it's time to refill.

  • Misting control

    Duration 2–20 s, interval 5–90 min. Emergency override in <1 s.

    Customize cycles per growth stage. One-tap "Mist now" from the app for instant delivery.

  • Wi‑Fi & sync

    2.4 GHz, auto-reconnect. Real-time app updates <20 s.

    Stays connected. If Wi‑Fi drops, the device keeps running and syncs when back online.

  • Local display

    2.8" LCD for temp, humidity, water level, cycle timers.

    Check status at a glance without opening the app. All key metrics on one screen.

  • Safety

    Pump off when water low; LEDs off above 40°C. Alerts in <15 s.

    Protection built in. You get push notifications so you can act before plants are affected.

  • Size & misting

    32 × 20 cm footprint · 56 cm tall · 6 misting nozzles.

    Dimensions: Compact for a counter or shelf—32 × 20 cm footprint, 56 cm height.

    Nozzles: Six misting nozzles spread fine mist evenly across the grow area.

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about AeroMist.

Aeroponics is a method of growing plants in an air or mist environment without soil. Unlike hydroponics (which uses water), aeroponics suspends plant roots in the air and delivers nutrients through a fine mist. This provides better oxygenation, uses 90% less water, and promotes 2x faster growth compared to traditional soil methods.

AeroMist is perfect for herbs (basil, mint, cilantro, parsley), leafy greens (lettuce, spinach, kale), and small vegetables (cherry tomatoes, peppers). Best results are with plants that have shallow to medium root systems. The system is optimized for culinary herbs and salad greens.
